About Us

Dr. P.Narayana an educationalist promoted this institute Narayana Engineering College in the year 1998 .The branch electronics and computers popularly known as ECM was started as a new branch from the very beginning of the college . This course equips students with hardware as well as software knowledge and aims to give them the ability to organize the hardware and software into the best of use. This course is versatile in its contents with the students studying the amalgamation of both the domains of Computers and Electronics. An hands on approach is adopted to improvise the practical methodology of students.
