Library

Centrally air conditioned library and information centre housed in B-Block first floor with carpet area of about 650 Sqm, and a seating capacity of 250. It has 30925 books with 6254 titles and also subscribes 110 national and 12 international journals and subscribed for on-line journals(IEEE). Back volumes of the journals and project reports of our students are available.

Library activities has been computerized using in-built online software and using Barcode system for the Circulation of books.

To meet ever growing necessity of the latest resources in engineering, digital library was established with 40 systems. It consists sonet vcds, nptel lectures,delnet and online journals(IEEE) access.

To know the information regarding new arrivals, users status and material details search systems are provided.

The students who are participating in the seminars, and discuss about their project work ,separate discussion rooms were designed.

To watch live technical channels like ekalavya and mana tv ,having audio visual theatre with jumbo screen , projector with 40 seating capacity.
